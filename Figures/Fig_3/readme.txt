We present the M-R and I-M relations for five representative NS EOSs: MS0, BSK21, ENG, APR, and WFF1.
First 10 columns are for M-R relation and last 10 columns are for M-I relation.
The name of EOS is in the header.

M: mass of NS, unit is solar mass;
R: radius of NS, unit is km;
I: rotational moment of NS, unit is 1e45 g cm^2.
